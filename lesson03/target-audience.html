Target Audience

Personas

Olha Kovalova

  • Occupation: Marketing Executive
  • Demographics and Education: 32 years old. Has a masters in Marketing and has now been at her company for 5 years.
  • Goals and motivation for using the site: Travels to Idaho often so she needs to stay updated with the weather in order to know what to pack.
  • Social:As mentioned before, she travels often and as a marketing executive is expected to socialize with important figures in the business world.
  • Technology: Has an iPhone 10 and a Dell laptop that she uses for everything. She mostly relies on her phone unless she is preparing an important document.

Brian Osorio

  • Occupation:Chief Executive Officer (CEO)
  • Demographics and Education:37 years old. Hospital head with a masters in Health Care Administration. He has been work for this hospital for 15 years and advanced to CEO just 2 years ago.
  • Goals and motivation for using the site: Has to drive 30 miles to work everyday. The weather affects his commute so he checks it daily.
  • Social:Does not often have free time to socialize, but he does frequently travel for big meetings with investors in the hospital.
  • Technology: Similar to Olha, Brian does not use a lot of techonology. He only has his iPhone 12 and his MacBook Pro. unlike Olha, he primarily uses his laptop and only uses his phone when he does not have his laptop in his immediate vicinity.
