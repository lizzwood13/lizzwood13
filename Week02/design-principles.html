University View

On this page when you immediately open up you see only one option: "Apply". Typically when someone is visiting this website that is the only thing they are really there to do.

Visual Hierarchy

Fat Cats

Fat Cats

Your eye moves from biggest to smallest element on this page. I think they do this on purpose to show off their deals before you get to what you visit the page for, which in this case is the movie showings.

Alignment

Love Olive Co.

Love Olive Co.

Since this is a clothing outlet, it makes sense to have grid style layout. The alignment of each individual element guides your eyes down they page, almost dragging you through the content.
